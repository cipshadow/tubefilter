## Seasonal Pattern

- **Heavy months:** June, December, March (~46 vids/week)
- **Light months:** November, August (~19 vids/week)
- December consistently worst: only 3 YouTube-free days in Dec 2025

## Key Insights

- 2024 Q2 was the inflection point where consumption jumped from ~25 to 42/week
- 73% of watch time comes from long videos (>20min), only 24% of clicks
- 28% of videos are under 2 min (shorts/clips) but account for just 1% of time
- Weekday and weekend consumption nearly identical (~70 min/day)
- Peak watching hour: 8-9pm UTC (9-10pm London)
- 16% of days have binge sessions (3+ videos from one channel)

## TubeFilter Hypothesis

By replacing the YouTube homepage with a curated weekly digest:
- Reduce algorithmic rabbit holes and autoplay binges
- Cut homepage visits (the main trigger for unplanned watching)
- Target: reduce from 30 vids/week to <20 vids/week
